Act 1: The General Play Act (VERSION 10/2/26)

 

By joining this world you agree to abide by the following enforced rules and regulations or else you will risk the following punishments. This is done to keep the experience fun for all. Repetitively breaking rules could land you a ban for the rest of the day or week if deemed necessary.

          

Beware that the local police department will be actively enforcing these rules at all times. You are also encouraged to report a crime if you see one being committed.

          

This is the first of a few acts to come focusing on different industries and topics, this one in particular focusing on general gameplay.

          

Rules will be under the [Act #]/[Rule #] format, so for example vandalism would be 1/1.

 

So to summarise, don’t be a dick and enjoy!

#

CRIME

CONSEQUENCE

1

Unpermitted vandalism (‘griefing’ without breaking anything, including but not limited to the act of ‘spamming’ blocks on another’s property), also includes developing on owned land without owners’ permission and also includes the modification of the affected building or flipping trapdoors that are designated to be immobile.

You will lose playing privileges for 6-20 minutes depending on the extent of the crime.

2

Criminal damage (traditional ‘griefing’ involving destruction)

You will lose playing privileges for 9-35 minutes depending on the extent of the crime. If it’s a permitted demolition from a demolitionist, that’s fine so long as no damage occurs outside of that owned land.

3

Entering a property without prior permission or remaining inside a property after being requested to leave (trespassing)

You will lose playing privileges for 3-10 minutes depending on the extent of the crime. The affected property owner may choose to temporarily or permanently ban you from their premises.

4

Pestering someone, this can include general harassment or the stoppage of projects of one or more people

You will lose playing privileges for 4-20 minutes depending on the extent of the crime.

5

Unjust murder of a player or other entity (incl. pets & livestock)

This will be resolved in a court case.

6

Fraudulent business operations

Your business will be at risk of closure and the offender may lose world access for 3-21 days depending on severity.

7

Fraudulent personal operations

The offender will face a loss world access for 1-28 days depending on severity.

8

MASS terraforming (incl. deforestation) and/or development of an area without prior government planning permission

Your development will be at risk of demolition and the offender may have to clean up or lose world access for 4-28 days depending on severity.

9

Intentional world lag/destruction, also including the summoning and/or activation of dangerous mobs including but not limited to withers, creepers and charged creepers.

You will lose playing permissions or be banned from anywhere from 6 days to indefinitely.

10

Impersonation of official person such as a ‘mayor’ or police officer

You will lose playing permissions or be banned from anywhere from 1 day to 14 days.

11

Abusing your role as an official person

You instantly lose your title and may face permission loss and/or a ban from 3 days to forever.

12

Theft

The items must be returned to the respectful owner and the fate of the thief will be decided in a court case.
